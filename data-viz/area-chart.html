Single Area Chart

Basic area chart with a filled region beneath the line. The fill uses reduced opacity to maintain readability of grid lines and axis labels.

Stacked Area Chart

Multiple data series stacked to show cumulative totals and part-to-whole relationships over time. Each area builds on the previous series. Uses 60% opacity fills for layering.

Multi-series stacked
Traffic by Source Sessions (thousands) — 12 months
Direct Organic Search Referral

Gradient Area Chart

Area chart with a vertical gradient fill that fades from the series color at the top to transparent at the baseline. Creates a softer visual effect for single-series trend data.
