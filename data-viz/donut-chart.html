Standard Donut Chart

Circular chart with a hollow center, used for showing part-to-whole relationships. The inner radius is approximately 60% of the outer radius. A center label displays the total or key metric.

4-segment donut

Semi-Donut Chart

A half-circle gauge visualization used for progress or goal tracking. Only the top 180° arc is rendered, with a center label showing the current value.

Half-circle gauge
Goal Progress Q1 revenue target

Small Donut / Sparkline

Compact donut charts (100px) for dashboard widgets and summary cards. Each uses two segments: a filled portion and a remaining track in warm-gray-100.
