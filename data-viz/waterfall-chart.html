Waterfall Chart

Sequential increases and decreases between a starting and ending value. Uses color-coded bars for totals, gains, and losses.

Waterfall Color Key

Waterfall charts use three semantic colors: a total bar color for start/end values, green for increases, and red for decreases. Floating bars are connected by dashed connector lines.

Standard Waterfall

Vertical waterfall chart showing how a starting value changes through sequential additions and subtractions to reach a final value. Total bars anchor to the baseline; intermediate bars float.
