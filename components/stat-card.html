Trend Variants

Trend indicators come in directional (--up, --down, --neutral) and semantic (--positive, --negative) variants. Context matters: a downward trend in violations is positive, while a downward trend in compliance is negative.

Semantic Trends

Use --positive and --negative to convey whether a trend is good or bad regardless of direction. "Open Issues down 31%" is positive (green), while "Customer Satisfaction down 5%" is negative (red).

Semantic trend meaning
Open Issues 23 trending_down 31% — down is good
Customer Satisfaction 87% trending_down 5% — down is bad
Active Users 3,842 trending_up 12% — up is good
Support Tickets 14 trending_up 22% — up is bad

Highlighted Variant

Cards with a colored left accent bar to categorize metrics by type. The accent renders inside the card using a pseudo-element with a straight right edge and a rounded left edge clipped by the card's overflow. Blue for informational, red for critical alerts, and green for health metrics.
