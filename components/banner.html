Banner

Full-width system-level announcements for maintenance windows, important updates, subscription changes, and organizational notices.

All Variants

Four semantic variants communicate the nature and urgency of the announcement. Banners span the full width of the viewport and stack at the top of the page.

Info / Warning / Error / Success
info
Scheduled maintenance: March 20, 2:00 AM – 4:00 AM EST. Some services may be temporarily unavailable.
warning
Subscription expiring: Your team plan expires in 14 days. Contact your administrator to renew.
error
Service disruption: 3 integrations require immediate attention. Review details in the Admin Console.
check_circle
Migration complete: All projects have been successfully migrated to the new infrastructure.

With Actions

Banners can include action buttons to direct users toward resolution. Actions are right-aligned and use small secondary buttons.

Dismissible

Banners with a close button allow users to acknowledge and dismiss the notification. The dismiss button uses a Material Symbols close icon.

Click the close icon to dismiss
info
Platform update completed successfully. No action required.
warning
Storage limit approaching: Your workspace has used 90% of available storage. Consider upgrading your plan.
error
Export failed: 2 scheduled reports could not be generated. Check your export settings.
check_circle
All team members have been migrated to the new workspace successfully.

Sticky Banner

Banners can be made sticky so they remain visible as users scroll through content. This pattern is used for persistent alerts that require ongoing attention.
