Chart Cards

Cards built around data visualizations — horizontal bars, donuts, area charts, multi-series lines, and vertical bars. Use when the chart is the primary content, not a supporting detail.

Horizontal bar breakdowns inside a card. Useful for showing distribution across categories like data types, departments, or risk levels.

Area Chart Cards

Cards wrapping a filled area chart with axis labels and an optional tooltip annotation. Shows trends over time with more visual weight than a sparkline.

Multi-Series Line Cards

Two or more line series plotted on the same axes with a legend to distinguish them. Use for comparing related metrics over the same time period — like scans vs. findings, or inbound vs. outbound data.
