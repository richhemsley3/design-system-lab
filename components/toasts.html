Toasts

Inline alert banners and floating toast notifications for communicating feedback, status changes, and system messages.

Inline Alerts

Full-width banners that appear within the page flow. Use for persistent messages that require user attention, such as form validation, warnings, or confirmation feedback.

Floating Toasts

Temporary notifications that appear in the top-right corner of the viewport. They slide in, stack vertically, and auto-dismiss after 5 seconds. Use for transient feedback like save confirmations or background task completion.
