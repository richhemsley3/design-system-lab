Tabs

Horizontal tab bars used to switch between content sections. Supports underline page tabs and pill-style toggle tabs.

Page Tabs

Horizontal tab bar used below page headings to switch between content sections. The active tab shows a 2 px bottom indicator in blue-750 with a full-width border below all tabs.
