Small Modal

This is a small modal (400px max-width). Best suited for simple confirmations, brief alerts, or single-action prompts that don't require much content space.

Medium Modal

This is the default medium modal (560px max-width). It provides enough room for standard forms, informational content, or multi-step confirmations without overwhelming the viewport.

Use this size for most dialog interactions in the application, including settings changes, resource creation, and team management tasks.

Large Modal

This is the large modal (720px max-width). Reserved for complex forms with multiple fields, data-heavy detail views, or content that benefits from additional horizontal space.

Common use cases include multi-step wizards, resource configuration panels, detailed comparison views, and bulk operation confirmations with expandable resource lists.

Avoid using the large size when a medium modal would suffice. Overly spacious modals can feel empty and reduce the sense of focus that dialogs are meant to provide.
