KPI & Metric Cards

Single-metric cards with supporting context — a trend line, progress ring, comparison target, or icon badge. Designed for dashboards where the primary content is a number.

Sparkline Cards

A metric paired with an inline sparkline showing the trend over time. The area fill provides visual weight while the trend pill communicates direction.
