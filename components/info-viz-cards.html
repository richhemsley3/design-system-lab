Info Visualization Cards

Compact visual scoring and comparison cards — gauges, radars, lollipops, and activity bars. Use when the visualization itself communicates a score, posture, or pattern rather than precise data.

Gauge Cards

Semi-circle gauge for displaying scores, health ratings, or performance metrics. The needle position and arc color communicate the value at a glance — ideal for security posture scores, SLA health, or compliance ratings.

Lollipop Chart Cards

Paired dots connected by a vertical stem, comparing two values per category — such as current vs. previous period, or actual vs. target. The visual gap between dots communicates the delta at a glance.

Radar Chart Cards

Multi-axis radar charts for visualizing scores across several dimensions simultaneously. Use for security posture assessments, maturity models, or capability comparisons where 4-6 factors need to be evaluated together.

Activity Bar Cards

Vertical rounded progress bars per day or period, with a track background showing capacity. Use for scan activity, login volume, or alert frequency over a week.
